I have the pleasure of knowing some of the most social, kind, and genuine people. This is a list of some of the qualities I really admire in them, and I imagine it'll only grow with time.

- Being magnanimous with one's connections. Having an abundance of friends and being willing to share them and introduce them to one another brings so much lovely cross-pollination, and I no longer believe in distinct friend groups. 
- Having unabashed, deep interest in at least one thing. This makes someone so, so interesting (and as a sidenote generally makes it easier to give them gifts)! It doesn't have to be rare, or difficult, or high-brow - just getting excited about a specific topic is enough. 
- Being purely curious about other people. To borrow an expression from a friend, to be easily 'nerd-sniped' is a sign of good personhood.
- Having a deep respect for learning and the pursuit of knowledge. Speaking well about other people is a given, speaking well about teachers and mentors and showing joy in learning is even better.
